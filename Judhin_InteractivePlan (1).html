Blood Sugar Balance Protocol

1
Savory Breakfast First — Protein + fat every morning. Never start the day with sugar, juice, or refined carbs. Non-negotiable for pre-diabetes management.
2
Green Starter Every Meal — Raw or cooked vegetables BEFORE the main course at lunch and dinner. Reduces glucose spike by up to 30%.
3
Vinegar Hack — 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar in 250ml water, 15 minutes before your largest meal. Reduces glucose spike by up to 30%.
4
Clothe Your Carbs — Never eat carbohydrates alone. Always pair with protein, fat, and fiber. Applies to snacks too.
5
Walk After Eating — A 10-minute walk after meals reduces your glucose spike by up to 37%. Especially important after lunch.
